FAQ

Lead generation calculator questions

Grounded assumptions, realistic benchmarks, and the metrics growth teams watch.

Which inputs matter most?

Landing conversion rate, lead qualification rate, and average deal value typically drive the largest swings in revenue. Traffic volume is a lever, but improving conversion quality usually produces faster ROI.

What is a healthy ROI?

High-performing demand engines target a 4–8x revenue-to-spend multiple with a CAC below 35–40% of average deal value. Use the calculator to stress-test scenarios before scaling spend.

How should I pick benchmarks?

If you lack historical data, start with 2–4% landing conversion, 25–45% lead-to-opportunity, and 15–25% close rate. Replace with your own metrics as soon as you have a 60–90 day sample.

Does this include outbound?

Yes—traffic can represent ad clicks, organic visits, or targeted outreach volume. Adjust the conversion rates to mirror your primary motion and compare blended outcomes.
